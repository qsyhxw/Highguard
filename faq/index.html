üéÆ Getting Started

What is Highguard?

Highguard is a free-to-play PvP raid shooter developed by Wildlight Entertainment. Teams of 3 battle across large maps to loot resources, upgrade their arsenal, and ultimately destroy the enemy base using the Shieldbreaker artifact.

The game blends tactical shooting with MOBA-like character abilities, featuring mounted combat, base building, and objective-focused gameplay.

Is Highguard free to play?
Yes, Highguard is completely free to play on all platforms (PC, PlayStation 5, and Xbox Series X|S). There's no upfront cost to download and play the game.
When was Highguard released?
Highguard was released on January 26, 2026 on PC, PlayStation 5, and Xbox Series X|S simultaneously.
Who developed Highguard?
Highguard was developed by Wildlight Entertainment, a studio focused on creating innovative multiplayer experiences.

What platforms is Highguard on?
Highguard is available on:
  • PC (via Steam)
  • PlayStation 5
  • Xbox Series X|S

Note: The game is not available on PS4, Xbox One, or Nintendo Switch.

Does Highguard have crossplay?
Yes! Highguard supports full crossplay between PC, PlayStation 5, and Xbox Series X|S. Crossplay is enabled by default, so you can play with friends on any platform right away.
Is there cross-progression?
Yes, Highguard supports cross-progression. You can link your accounts across platforms to carry your progress, unlocks, and cosmetics between PC, PlayStation, and Xbox.

Why does Highguard require TPM 2.0?

‚öîÔ∏è Gameplay

How long is a Highguard match?
Matches typically last between 8 to 30 minutes, depending on how aggressively teams play and how evenly matched they are.
What is the team size?
Standard matches are 3v3 (two teams of three players). There's also an experimental 5v5 mode for larger battles.
How do you win in Highguard?
To win, you need to destroy the enemy base:
  1. Secure the Shieldbreaker artifact
  2. Carry it to the enemy base to drop their shields
  3. Raid and destroy their generators
  4. Destroy the Anchor Stone for instant victory
What are Wardens?
Wardens are the playable characters in Highguard. Each has unique abilities, passives, and ultimates. The game launched with 8 Wardens across different archetypes: Assault, Defense, Destruction, Recon, and Support.

See our Warden Tier List for rankings and guides.

üîß Game Mechanics

What is Vesper?
Vesper is the in-game currency collected during matches. Get it by:
  • Mining glowing crystals
  • Opening chests scattered across the map
  • Completing objectives

Use Vesper to upgrade weapons, buy armor, and purchase items at shops.

What is the Shieldbreaker?
The Shieldbreaker is a legendary artifact that spawns at a central location on the map. Teams fight to control it. Carrying it to the enemy base disables their protective shields, allowing you to raid and destroy their generators.
What are Soul Orbs?
When players die, they leave behind Soul Orbs:
  • Red (enemy): Destroy to delay their respawn
  • Green (ally): Collect to revive your teammate

Purging enemy souls also repairs 2 bars of your armor!

üíª Technical & Settings

What anti-cheat does Highguard use?
Highguard uses Easy Anti-Cheat (EAC), a kernel-level anti-cheat system. On PC, this requires Secure Boot and TPM 2.0 to be enabled in your BIOS settings.
What are the best competitive settings?
For competitive advantage:
  • FOV: Max (110) for better peripheral vision
  • Motion Blur: Off for clearer visuals
  • Auto-run: On for faster movement
