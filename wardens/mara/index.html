üí°Tips & Strategies

Always Sacrifice Soul Orbs

Every enemy kill is a free Soul Armor recharge. Never leave orbs unfinished.

Pre-Shield Before Fights

Apply Soul Armor to teammates before they engage. The overshield can win trades.

Immortal Coil Placement

Place spawn point in safe but close positions during raids. Near cover, not in the open.

Stay With Your Team

Mara is weak solo. Stick with teammates to maximize your support value.
