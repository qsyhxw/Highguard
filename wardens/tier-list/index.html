S-TIER

"A trained assassin who wields wind and sand to outflank opponents and infiltrate enemy bases."

Scarlet is Highguard's premier flanker and assassin. Her Shifting Sands passive is arguably the most powerful utility in the game, allowing her to phase through destructible walls. Combined with stealth capabilities, she can infiltrate enemy bases undetected and plant bombs before defenders realize she's there.

Shifting Sands: Shift a destructible wall into sand, temporarily making it passable.
TACTICAL
Sandstorm: Enter stealth mode, making yourself hidden to enemies that are far away.
ULTIMATE
Whirling Blades: Throw up to 6 deadly daggers. Killing a player with a dagger resets your cooldowns.

ASSAULT ‚Ä¢ Lightning Captain
S-TIER

"A lightning-wielding captain who uses a high-voltage arsenal to control the battlefield and wreak havoc upon the enemy."

Atticus is Highguard's poster character and an extremely versatile Assault Warden. His Supercharged Axe speeds up Vesper farming for faster gear upgrades. ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Who is the best Warden in Highguard?
Based on early meta consensus, Scarlet and Atticus are considered the best Wardens. Scarlet's infiltration abilities make her essential for raids, while Atticus provides unmatched versatility across all phases of a match.
How many Wardens are in Highguard?
Highguard launched with 8 Wardens: Atticus, Condor, Kai, Mara, Redmane, Scarlet, Slade, and Una. More Wardens will be added in future Episode updates.
Are all Wardens free in Highguard?
Yes, all Wardens in Highguard are completely free. Unlike some hero shooters, you do not need to unlock or purchase any characters.
What are the 5 Warden classes?
The 5 Warden classes are: Assault (aggressive combat), Defensive (protection and repairs), Destruction (breaking structures), Recon (scouting and intel), and Support (healing and team buffs).
When will this tier list be updated?
We update this tier list with each Episode release, major balance patches, and as the competitive meta evolves. The next major update is expected with Episode 2 in February 2026.
Which Warden is best for beginners?
Atticus is recommended for beginners due to his straightforward abilities and versatility. Kai is also beginner-friendly for players who prefer a defensive playstyle.
