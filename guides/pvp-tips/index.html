2 Movement Techniques

Highguard's movement is described as "snappy, sticky, and a whole lot of fun" by players. Mastering movement gives you a significant combat advantage.

üõπ

Hop Slide

Essential movement tech

The hop slide is a momentum technique that lets you move faster and become harder to hit. Originally a bug, it was kept because it adds depth to gameplay.

Works: On foot, while mounted, and when dismounting from ziplines
ü™ù

Zipline Mastery

Quick map traversal

Ziplines provide rapid vertical and horizontal movement. Use them for flanking, retreating, and reaching elevated positions. Combine with hop slide on dismount for momentum.

Mounted Combat

Mobile gunfighting

Mounts aren't just for travel‚Äîyou can shoot while riding. Use mounts for drive-by attacks, quick rotations, and escape. But remember: mounts are destructible and loud. Know when to dismount.

üí° Pro Tip: Movement is your best defense. A moving target is harder to hit than one behind cover. Combine strafing, sliding, and jumping to become unpredictable.

5 Soul Orb Mechanics

Soul Orbs are a critical but often overlooked mechanic that can swing fights in your favor.

üíÄ Enemy Soul Orbs

When enemies die, they drop Soul Orbs. Destroy them to delay their respawn timer significantly.

Tip: Always finish Soul Orbs after kills during raids

üíö Friendly Soul Orbs

When teammates die, collect their Soul Orbs to enable their revival. Protect friendly orbs during fights.

Tip: Reviving maintains team pressure during raids

6 Optimal Settings

Competitive settings can give you an edge. Here are recommended optimizations:

üéÆ Recommended Settings

FOV: 110 (max)
Motion Blur: Off
Response Curve (Controller): Exponential
Extra Turn Rate (ADS): Off
üí° Why FOV 110? Higher FOV provides better peripheral vision, letting you spot flanking enemies earlier. The trade-off is targets appear slightly smaller at distance.

7 Team Play

Highguard is a team game. Solo plays rarely win matches. Here's how to maximize team effectiveness:

üé§ Communication

Voice chat > ping system. Call out enemy positions, Shieldbreaker timing, and coordinate abilities. If no voice, use pings constantly.

‚öîÔ∏è Team Composition

Balance your Warden picks. Include at least one damage dealer, one support/defensive Warden, and consider map-specific picks.

üéØ Focus Fire

Call targets and focus one enemy at a time. Three players shooting one target wins faster than splitting damage across three enemies.

üîÑ Trade Kills

If a teammate takes a fight, be ready to trade. Staying close enough to revenge kill means their death isn't wasted.

8 Frequently Asked Questions

What are Soul Orbs in Highguard?
Soul Orbs drop when players die. Destroying enemy Soul Orbs delays their respawn, while collecting friendly Soul Orbs allows you to revive downed teammates.
What is the hop slide in Highguard?
The hop slide is a movement technique that provides momentum. It can be performed on foot, while mounted, and when dismounting from ziplines. Originally a bug, it was kept due to its gameplay value.
What FOV should I use in Highguard?
Most competitive players recommend maxing FOV to 110 for better peripheral vision. Also consider disabling motion blur for clearer visuals during fast movement.
What is the best weapon in Highguard?
The Saber Rifle is currently considered top-tier due to its high damage potential (especially headshots) and effectiveness at both close and long range.
Is there health regeneration in Highguard?
Yes, there's passive health regeneration when you're out of combat. This makes disengaging from losing fights a valid strategy‚Äî heal up and re-engage on your terms.
