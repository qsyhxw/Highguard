1 What is Highguard?

Highguard is a free-to-play PvP raid shooter developed by Wildlight Entertainment. Released on January 26, 2026 for PC (Steam), the game features 3v3 team battles where players take on the role of Wardens‚Äîarcane gunslingers fighting for control of a mythical continent.

Unlike traditional shooters, Highguard combines gunplay with base building, mounted exploration, and raid mechanics. Each match is a dynamic cycle of looting, battling for objectives, and ultimately destroying the enemy team's base.

üí° Key Takeaway: Think of Highguard as a blend of Hunt: Showdown's atmosphere, Rust's raiding, and hero shooter mechanics‚Äîall in a fantasy setting.

2 Core Game Loop

Every Highguard match follows a structured loop. Understanding this flow is crucial for new players:

Phase 1: Setup

Choose your Warden, select a Stronghold (base), and reinforce your defenses. Customize your loadout before heading out.

Phase 2: Exploration & Looting

Mount up and ride across the map. Gather upgraded weapons, armor, amulets, and saddles. The better your gear, the stronger you'll be in fights.

Phase 3: Shieldbreaker Battle

When magical storms appear, a legendary sword called the Shieldbreaker spawns at its center. Both teams fight to claim it.

Phase 4: The Raid

Carry the Shieldbreaker to the enemy base, summon a Siege Tower to break their shields, then infiltrate and plant bombs on their generators or anchor stone.

Phase 5: Victory or Reset

Destroy the enemy base to win. If the raid fails, shields regenerate, gear escalates, and a new Shieldbreaker spawns elsewhere.

5 How Raids Work

Raiding is the heart of Highguard. Here's the step-by-step breakdown:

Step Action
1 Wait for a magical storm to appear on the map
2 Race to the storm's center to claim the Shieldbreaker
3 Fight the enemy team‚Äîif you die carrying it, they can pick it up
4 Carry the Shieldbreaker to the enemy base perimeter
5 Plunge the sword into their outer shield to weaken it
6 Summon a Siege Tower to fully break the shields
7 Breach the base using guns, raid tools, or arcane abilities
8 Plant bombs on generators or destroy the anchor stone directly

6 Your First Match: 5 Essential Tips

1. Prioritize Looting Early

Better gear = better odds. Spend the first few minutes gathering upgraded weapons and armor before engaging.

2. Stay With Your Team

3v1 fights are unwinnable. Always move and fight as a unit. Coordinate via voice chat if possible.

3. Learn to Mount Quickly

Mobility is key. Practice mounting and dismounting smoothly to escape or reposition.

4. Watch the Storm

When the magical storm appears, be ready to contest or position strategically. Don't ignore it.

5. Defend When Needed

If the enemy has the Shieldbreaker, fall back to base. Repel the raid until their Siege Tower energy depletes.

8 Frequently Asked Questions

What is Highguard?
Highguard is a free-to-play PvP raid shooter by Wildlight Entertainment. Teams of 3 Wardens battle for control of a mythical continent through looting, objective fights, and base raids.
Is Highguard completely free?
Yes, all core content is free. Monthly Episode updates add new Wardens, weapons, maps, and modes at no cost.
Can I play solo?
Highguard is designed for 3-player teams. You can queue solo and be matched with teammates, but coordinated squads have an advantage.
What are the PC requirements?
Minimum: Windows 10, i5-6600K, 8GB RAM, GTX 1060, 25GB SSD.
Recommended: Windows 11, i5-9600K, 12GB RAM, RTX 2080, NVMe SSD.
