1 What is Highguard?

Highguard is a free-to-play PvP raid shooter developed by Wildlight Entertainment‚Äîa studio founded by veterans who worked on Apex Legends and Titanfall. Released on January 26, 2026 for PC (Steam), PlayStation 5, and Xbox Series X/S, the game features 3v3 team battles where players take on the role of Wardens‚Äîarcane gunslingers fighting for control of a mythical continent.

Unlike traditional shooters, Highguard combines gunplay with base building, mounted exploration, and raid mechanics. Each match is a dynamic cycle of looting, battling for the legendary Shieldbreaker sword, and ultimately destroying the enemy team's base.

The game supports full cross-play and cross-progression across all platforms, meaning you can team up with friends regardless of their system and carry your progress everywhere.

üí° Key Takeaway: Think of Highguard as a blend of Hunt: Showdown's atmosphere, Rust's raiding, and hero shooter mechanics‚Äîall wrapped in a fantasy setting with mounted combat.

2 Core Game Loop

Every Highguard match follows a structured loop with distinct phases. Understanding this flow is crucial for new players:

1

Reinforce Phase

Choose your Warden, select a Stronghold (base), and reinforce up to 5 walls. Customize your starting loadout with two weapons and one raid tool before heading out.

2

Gear Up Phase

Mount up and ride across the map. Gather upgraded weapons, armor, amulets, and saddles. Mine Vesper (the in-game currency) from nodes and trade with the vendor Flynn for better gear.

3

Shieldbreaker Battle

When magical storms appear, a legendary sword called the Shieldbreaker spawns at its center. Both teams fight to claim it‚Äîthis is the pivotal moment of every match.

4

The Raid

Carry the Shieldbreaker to the enemy base and plunge it into their shields. This summons a Siege Tower as your respawn point. Breach the base and plant bombs on generators or destroy the Anchor Stone.

5

Victory or Escalation

Destroy the enemy base (reduce health to 0) to win. If the raid fails, shields regenerate, loot quality escalates, and a new Shieldbreaker spawns elsewhere. The cycle continues until one base falls.

‚ö†Ô∏è Match Length: Matches can vary from 7 minutes (one-sided domination) to over 30 minutes if teams trade raids back and forth. Each base has 100 health at the start.

5 How Raids Work

Raiding is the heart of Highguard. Here's the step-by-step breakdown:

Step Action
1 Wait for a magical storm to appear on the map
2 Race to the storm's center to claim the Shieldbreaker
3 Fight the enemy team‚Äîif you die carrying it, they can pick it up
4 Carry the Shieldbreaker to the enemy base perimeter (carrier is visible on map)
5 Plunge the sword into their outer shield to weaken it (deals 30 damage)
6 A Siege Tower spawns as your forward respawn point
7 Breach using guns, raid tools (Blast Hammer, Rocket Launcher), or abilities
8 Plant bombs on generators (2 total) OR destroy the Anchor Stone directly

üéØ Anchor Stone

Destroying the Anchor Stone instantly wins the match but it's usually the most heavily defended spot.

‚öôÔ∏è Generators

There are 2 generators. Destroying both wins, or damaging one chips away at base health. Often the safer strategy.

üí° Pro Tip: If defenders eliminate all attackers or the Siege Tower energy depletes, the raid fails. Shields repair, loot escalates, and a new Shieldbreaker spawns elsewhere.

6 Your First Match: 7 Essential Tips

1. Prioritize Looting Early‚ÄîBut Not Too Long

Better gear = better odds. Spend the first minutes gathering weapons and armor, but stop when you have blue armor and a decent weapon. Don't miss the Shieldbreaker spawn.

2. Stay With Your Team

3v1 fights are unwinnable. In a 3v3 format, every player matters. Always move and fight as a unit‚Äîcoordinate via voice chat if possible.

3. Master Mount Mechanics

Mounts aren't just transport‚Äîthey're tactical tools. Practice mounting/dismounting smoothly. Bind mount summon to an easily accessible key.

4. Watch the Storm

When the magical storm appears, be ready to rotate. Position near the Shieldbreaker spawn with time to spare‚Äîdon't be caught looting.

5. Know Your Role

If the enemy has the Shieldbreaker, consider whether to intercept or fall back to defend. Sometimes setting up an ambush route beats a direct chase.

6. Use Raid Tools Effectively

Bring a Blast Hammer for walls or Rocket Launcher for structures. Coordinate with teammates‚Äîuse tools in combination with abilities for maximum impact.

7. Don't Give Up After One Failed Raid

If your raid fails, the match isn't over. Loot escalates each cycle. Regroup, gear up again, and prepare for the next Shieldbreaker spawn.

9 Frequently Asked Questions

What is Highguard?
Highguard is a free-to-play PvP raid shooter by Wildlight Entertainment. Teams of 3 Wardens battle for control of a mythical continent through looting, objective fights, and base raids. Available on PC, PS5, and Xbox Series X/S.
Is Highguard completely free?
Yes, all core content is free. Monthly Episode updates add new Wardens, weapons, maps, and modes at no cost. The game has cosmetic-only monetization with no pay-to-win mechanics.
How many Wardens are in Highguard?
Highguard launched with 8 Wardens: Redmane, Condor, Atticus, Kai, Slade, Scarlet, Una, and Mara. A new Warden is added approximately every two months with Episode updates.
Can I play solo?
Highguard is designed for 3-player teams. You can queue solo and be matched with teammates, but coordinated squads have a significant advantage due to the communication-heavy gameplay.
Is there cross-play?
Yes, Highguard supports full cross-play across PC, PS5, and Xbox Series X/S. Cross-progression is also enabled, so your progress carries between platforms.
What are the PC requirements?
Minimum: Windows 10, i5-6600K, 8GB RAM, GTX 1060, 25GB SSD.
Recommended: Windows 11, i5-9600K, 12GB RAM, RTX 2080, NVMe SSD.
Note: Requires Secure Boot and TPM 2.0 enabled.
What is the Shieldbreaker?
The Shieldbreaker is a legendary sword that spawns during magical storms. Teams fight to control it because it's the only way to break enemy base shields and initiate a raid. The carrier is visible on the map.
When will Ranked Mode release?
Ranked Mode is confirmed for Episode 2 Part 1, expected in February 2026. The roadmap shows 7 content updates planned throughout 2026.
