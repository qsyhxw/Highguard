1 Stronghold Overview

In Highguard, strongholds are pre-designed bases that teams select at the start of each match. Unlike traditional base-building games, you don't construct from scratch‚Äîinstead, you choose from a selection of unique bases, each with distinct layouts, advantages, and vulnerabilities.

After selecting your stronghold, you enter a ~1 minute preparation phase. This is your window to fortify defenses and coordinate with teammates.

Prep Phase Checklist

‚úì

‚úì

‚úì

4 Fortification Strategies

Smart fortification isn't just about reinforcing everything‚Äîit's about creating advantages for your team while limiting attacker options.

üî® Reinforce Generator Walls

Prioritize walls protecting bomb sites and generators. Extended breach time gives defenders more opportunity to intercept attackers.

üí• Break Ceilings Above Bomb Sites

Create openings in ceilings above bomb plant locations. This allows defenders to attack from above while enemies are planting.

üå≤ Clear Exterior Sightlines

Cut down trees and destroy cover around your base perimeter to spot attackers earlier and deny approach cover.

üöÄ Create Quick-Return Paths

Break walls between spawn and key positions to enable faster redeployment. Add ziplines from spawn to power positions.

ü™§ Set Ability Traps

Position Warden abilities strategically. Atticus's lightning above doors, Kai's ice walls at chokepoints, Una's wisps in corridors.

üí° Pro Tip: Balance reinforcement with sightlines. Sometimes breaking walls creates better defensive angles than reinforcing them.

8 Frequently Asked Questions

How many strongholds are in Highguard Episode 1?
There are 6 unique strongholds: Wellspring, Smuggler's Den, King's Lair, Hellmouth, Frosthold, and Bellringer. Each has distinct layouts and strategic characteristics.
Can you build bases from scratch in Highguard?
No. Teams select from pre-designed strongholds rather than building from scratch. You can modify them during the preparation phase by reinforcing walls, breaking ceilings/floors, and setting up ability traps.
How long is the preparation phase?
The preparation phase lasts approximately 1 minute, giving players time to reinforce walls, set loadouts, and familiarize themselves with the base layout.
Which Warden is best for base defense?
Kai is considered S-Tier for defense due to his free wall repair passive and Ice Wall ability. Una and Atticus are also strong A-Tier defensive options with their area denial and trap capabilities.
How long do I have to defuse a bomb?
Based on community testing, bombs have approximately a 20-second window for defusal. Prioritize defusing over chasing kills when bombs are planted.
