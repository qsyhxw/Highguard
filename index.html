Frequently Asked Questions

What is Highguard?
Highguard is a free-to-play PvP raid shooter by Wildlight Entertainment where 3-player teams fight as Wardens‚Äîarcane gunslingers battling for control of a mythical continent.
Is Highguard free to play?
Yes, Highguard is completely free to play on Steam with all core content‚Äîincluding new Episodes with maps, Wardens, and weapons‚Äîavailable at no cost.
What platforms is Highguard on?
Highguard is currently available on PC via Steam, requiring Windows 10 or Windows 11.
How many players per team?
Highguard features 3v3 team-based gameplay with each team consisting of three Wardens.
What are Wardens?
Wardens are the playable characters in Highguard‚Äîarcane gunslingers with unique magical abilities designed for roles like raiding, stealth, defense, harvesting, and combat.
How do raids work?
Teams fight for the Shieldbreaker sword spawned during magical storms, carry it to enemy bases to lower shields, summon Siege Towers, then breach and destroy generators or the anchor stone to win.
How often does Highguard update?
Highguard releases new Episodes monthly with new maps, Wardens, weapons, mounts, raid tools, and modes‚Äîall completely free for players.
What are the system requirements?
Minimum: Windows 10, Intel i5-6600K / Ryzen R5 1600, 8GB RAM, GTX 1060 / RX 580, 25GB SSD.
Recommended: Windows 11, i5-9600K / Ryzen 5 3600, 12GB RAM, RTX 2080 / RX 6650 XT, NVMe SSD.
