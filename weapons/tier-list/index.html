9 Frequently Asked Questions

How many weapons are in Highguard at launch?
Highguard launches with 13 weapons total: 10 combat weapons (3 Assault Rifles, 2 Shotguns, 2 SMGs, 1 Sniper, 1 Revolver, 1 Gatling Gun) plus 3 Raid Tools (Blast Hammer, Zipline Gun, Rocket Launcher).
What is the best weapon in Highguard?
Vanguard is the most versatile and beginner-friendly assault rifle. Kraken dominates close-quarters combat. Paladin has the highest raw DPS. Big Rig excels at suppression but only spawns in Care Packages.
Can any weapon one-shot kill?
No weapon can reliably one-shot full-health players. Ranger sniper headshots deal 148 damage‚Äîhigh but not a one-shot. Kraken shotgun can deal 150 damage at point-blank with all pellets hitting, but this requires perfect aim.
How do I get the Big Rig Gatling Gun?
Big Rig only spawns in Care Package Drops, which begin appearing during the second Gear Up phase. It cannot be purchased from shops or found in regular chests.
What is Vesper and how do I get it?
Vesper is the in-match currency used at shops to buy weapons, armor, and upgrades. You earn Vesper by mining resource nodes with your melee weapon (Axe) or by defeating enemy players.
Will more weapons be added?
Yes. Wildlight Entertainment has confirmed monthly Episode updates that include new weapons, Wardens, maps, and game modes. New content is free for all players.
